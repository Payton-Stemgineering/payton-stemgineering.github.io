About

At Payton Stemgineering, we believe that innovation starts with curiosity—and we’re here to help students turn that curiosity into action.

We are a student-led organization at Walter Payton College Prep dedicated to exploring the world of STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) through hands-on learning, collaborative projects, and creative problem-solving. Our nonprofit is a space where students of all backgrounds and experience levels can come together to learn, build, and imagine a better future through STEM.

Our Purpose

Our goal is to make STEM feel approachable, exciting, and open to everyone—no matter their background or experience level. We want students to feel like they belong in science, technology, engineering, and math, and to see that these fields are full of creativity, collaboration, and real-world opportunities. Through hands-on events, fun workshops, engineering challenges, guest speakers, and outreach programs, we give students the chance to explore what excites them, try new things, and connect what they’re learning in the classroom to real problems and possibilities in the world around them. Whether someone’s just starting to get curious about STEM or already passionate about it, we’re here to support their journey and help them see what’s possible!

Who We Are

We’re a student-run nonprofit led by a team of passionate students who bring STEM to life through hands-on learning and creativity. Our goal is to make STEM accessible and engaging for everyone, and we’re always finding new ways to share that excitement with our community!

Stemgineering at Walter Payton

Founded and based at Walter Payton College Prep, our club brings together over 60 members who meet regularly to explore STEM through creative, hands-on projects. From building challenges and chemistry experiments to seasonal workshops and design days, our meetings give members a chance to experiment, learn new skills, and collaborate with friends in a relaxed, engaging environment. Each meeting is a chance to try new things, learn alongside friends, and explore how creativity and problem-solving fit into STEM!
