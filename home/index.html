Payton Stemgineering

Inspiring the Next Generation of Innovators

Payton Stem Engineering is a student-led club at Walter Payton College Prep, dedicated to exploring STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Math) through hands-on projects, competitions, and mentorship. We aim to inspire the next generation of engineers and problem-solvers by making STEM accessible and engaging for all students.
